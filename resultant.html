Polynomial Resultant Calculator

Enter two polynomials with coefficients in Q, and press "Submit."
When entering non-integer coefficients, please use parenthesis and imperfect fractional form
(zB. "x^4+(3/4)x^3-4x^2+x-5")
The program will calculate the resultant of the two polynomials.

Apology: this can't handle every polynomial yet. If it spits out a bunch of oddly formatted numbers,
contrats, you've hit the jackpot. Try switching the "first" and "second" polynomials (then you'll just have to
multiply by negative one as appropriate) -- sometimes I haven't finished cross-compatibility, but chances are,
you'll just have to go figure it out yourself :P.
