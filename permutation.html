Permutation Powers Calculator

Enter a permutation in cyclic notation using spaces between elements of a cycle and parenthesis to designate cycles, and press "Submit."
[Eg. "(1 2 3 4 5)(6 7)".] The program will calculate the powers of the permutation.
That is, it calculates the cyclic subgroup of S_n generated by the element you entered.
